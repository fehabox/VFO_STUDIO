Core Libraries
----------------------------------------------------------

LovyanGFX  
  Author: lovyan03 and contributors  
  URL: https://github.com/lovyan03/LovyanGFX  
  Purpose: High-performance TFT display driver framework.

Etherkit Si5351 Arduino Library  
  Author: Jason Milldrum (KB9JHU)  
  URL: https://github.com/etherkit/si5351  
  Purpose: Si5351 PLL clock generator control.

Encoder Handling (Quadrature Algorithms)  
  Various sources, Arduino community contributions.  
  Used for stable EC11 and optical encoder tuning.
